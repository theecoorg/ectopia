Welcome to Ectopia.

Blueprint for future of civilization.

Mission

To preserve earth’s natural resources and build sustainable habitats.

Vision

A template for the habitats of the future.

Design & Engineering

Designed and engineered to be built in the ocean without the need for land reclamation. Structures will use steel and concrete with bioengineered sea walls to be resilient to natural disasters. The framework will be modular and can be laid out to represent any formation.

Food & Resources

Plant based food and fish will be the main source of food. Fresh water will be produced through atmospheric water generation and desalination. Automated indoor, vertical and hydroponic farming will guarantee year round harvest. Biowaste will be recycled to produce design materials for manufacturing.

Society & Governance

Communities will be designed around a skill based society. A digital system built on blockchain will manage the community administration, decision making and will use a direct democratic system for governance.

The community will be governed by different layers of governance, councils and associations with representatives at the city and neighborhood levels. A reputation system will be put into place to monitor and rate each member’s contribution to the society.

** The ecosystem can be built as an autonmous governing community or within an existing governing system.

Health, Wellness & Liveability

Food, shelter, equality, health, education and other social benefits will be guaranteed for all members of the community. The healthcare system will use precision medicine, robotics and AI. The education system will be built around project based education.

Nature reserves will be dedicated for each city that can support marine and wildlife, these reserves will double as forests. A leisure island with artificial beaches, lagoons, parks and other facilities will be dedicated for each city.

Manufacturing & Fashion

Plastics, metal, glass and biowaste will be recycled and used to design manufacturing materials. Everything from building materials, clothing, to household objects will be produced using sustainable materials. Innovative methods like 3D printing, nanotechnology, IoT will be utilized for manufacturing. The ecosystem will have designated industrial zones with production facilities.

Infrastructure, Energy & Technology

Different forms of renewable energy: solar, wind, wave energy will be used to power the ecosystem. A system powered by AI will manage the infrastructure and resources within the ecosystem. Utility robots and humanoids will be used for repetitive tasks and for more efficiency.

Transport & Mobility

Zero emission autonomous systems will transport cargo and passengers. Flying cars, drones, hyperloop systems will form the transportation network. Satellite network connections will be used to access the internet, giving the community full location independence.

The Economics

The economy will be built on a crypto economic system. Ectopian communities will have their own cryptocurrency pegged to valuables like gold or fiat currencies which will also be used to conduct business with external parties. Exports can include produce, manufactured goods, research or professional services. The community will be connected to the mainland via a Hyperloop System.

The controlled farming environments and self-sustainable nature of the Ectopian society gives it the advantage of business continuity planning which increases the possibilities for ROI.
